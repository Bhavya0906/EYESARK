About EYESARK:

Eyesark Ad Tech Private Limited is a Private Limited Indian Non-Government Company incorporated in India on 17 October 2023. Its registered office is in Surat, Gujarat, India.
At Eyes Media, we believe that digital signage is the future of advertising. Our digital display panels (DDPs) are strategically placed in high-traffic areas, such as shopping malls, airports, and business centers, to ensure maximum exposure for your brand.
Jenish Akbari and Yogesh Shekhda serve as directors at the Company.
